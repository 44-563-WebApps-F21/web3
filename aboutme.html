ANTMAN

The original Ant-Man was Biophysicist and Security Operations Center expert Dr. Henry 'Hank' Pym.
Hank was knocked unconscious and his wife Maria was murdered.Pym was greatly distraught by his wife's death, and decided to do whatever he could in the future to battle injustice.
After discovering a chemical substance, which he called Pym Particles, that would allow the user to alter his size, he armed himself with a helmet that could control ants
